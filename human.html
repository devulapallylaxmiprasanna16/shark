Quick Info Blocks

What’s happening out there

Sharks migrate across oceans following food sources and seasonal patterns; human activities can influence their behavior, so informed management is essential.

Stay safe in the water

Respect warning signs, stay away from feeding frenzies, and don’t attempt to touch or chase sharks.

How science helps

Satellite data, tagging, and citizen science help map shark movements and protect critical habitats.

Be part of the solution

Learn, share accurate information, and support conservation programs in coastal regions.
